HexaGrow

C# | Unity

HexaGrow is a turn-based strategy game on a hexagonal grid.

By collecting and selling their resources in the most optimal way (depending on the prices on the market), players can expand their empire to the top of the map and win the game.

This game was my first experience with Unity and C#.
