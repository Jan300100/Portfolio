Blood N' Justice

C# | Unity

This game was made as part of the Spooky 2D Game Jam in October 2022 together with 2 other developers.

You play as a vampire, collecting blood from patients in a hospital, and selling it to the bartender in a vampire bar. With the money you earn, you can buy cool hats :D
