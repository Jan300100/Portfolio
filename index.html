Professional Projects

Returnal PC Features Trailer

RETURNAL PC - Sony

UE4 | C++ | DirectX12

I feel very grateful to have been given the chance to work on the PC port of Housemarques PS5 exclusive 'Returnal' at Climax Studios as part of the Graphics and Optimization team. I am very proud of the work my coworkers and I did on this title and I am excited for its release.

This project has taught me a lot about Unreal Engine, The code behind it and how it can be extended, modified and extended.

I worked on fixing issues with Shaders and Loading times, optimisations, adding new material nodes and tools to help development, working on various systems such as the VRS, FSR and NIS implementations and fixing several gameplay and multiplayer bugs.
