Temple Run

Made with DirectX11 and C++ in the Overlord Engine,
this game is an Endless runner, based on the famous game temple run.
The player runs on the ruins of an ancient temple in a beautiful forest, avoiding any obstacles on the jungle path!

Challenges were the several graphical features in the game, like shadow mapping, post processing and instanced rendering, Because this was my first real DirectX project.

OhCrab!

Oh Crab is a co-op couch game where each player controls a crab on the beach.
The goal? Build a sand castle and protect your pearl from both the waves and the other crab.

This game was coded in C# in Unity. The biggest challenge was making sure the beach, the water and the crab reacted to each other in realistic and fun ways. It was made in a team of 5 people, of which 3 artists and 2 programmers. I was 1 of the programmers.

You can download OhCrab! for free on Itch.io!

Eagle Eye

This game was made for the Tobii C4 eye-tracking device and makes full advantage of its features.

These Features are mapping the player’s head position to the eagle’s head,
HUD-Elements that disappear when you are not looking for it and of course the main mechanic of the game : Catching pray with your eyes to feed and grow the eagle.

This Game was coded in Unity, together with a friend.

HexaGrow

HexaGrow was my first experience with Unity and C#.

It is a turn-based strategy game on a hexagonal grid, where each player controls their very own empire.

Each player tries to expand their empire to reach the top of the map, by collecting and selling their resources in the most optimal way, depending on the prices of these resources on the market.

NPCity

NPCity is a really small project where I simulated a lot of NPCs living their life.
Each of them has his own room, a job and a favorite bar or restaurant in one of the skyscrapers.
All of them have their own unique day and week schedules.

It was made in Unity.
