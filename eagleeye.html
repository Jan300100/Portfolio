Eagle Eye

C# | Unity | Tobii C4

This game was made for the Tobii C4 eye-tracking device and takes full advantage of its possibilities, like mapping the player’s head position to the eagle’s head, HUD-Elements that disappear when you are not looking for them and of course the main mechanic of the game : Catching pray with your eyes to feed and level-up the eagle.

This Game was made together with a friend.
